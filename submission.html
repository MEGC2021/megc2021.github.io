Submission guideline

Submission Website:

https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ACMMM2021/Submission/Index. If you do not have the account yet, you will need to sign up for an account to submit papers.

Papers and results for the Grand Challenge:

Multimedia grand challenge papers will go through a single-blind review process. Submitted papers (.pdf format) must use the ACM Article Template https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template as used by regular ACMMM submissions. Please limit each grand challenge paper submission to 4 pages with 1-2 extra pages for references only. Papers will be handled by the same submission site as the main track, while under the "Multimedia Grand Challenge Solutions" track. (https://2021.acmmm.org/multimedia-grand-challenge-submissions)


Before the final submission, please select the option : "Facial Micro-expression (FME) Challenge"


Papers for the workshop:

Workshop paper format should adhere to the paper submission guidelines for ACM Multimedia 2021 (https://2021.acmmm.org/regular-papers). On the submission website, please choose "1st workshop of Facial Micro-Expression: Advanced techniques for Facial Expressions Generation and Spotting" track to submit.
