Facial Micro-Expression (FME) Workshop and Challenge 2021

Advanced techniques for Facial Expressions Generation and Spotting




Update

* The FME'21 workshop will take place on October 24th, 2021, 13:00-17:30 pm (UTC+8), and the workshop program is available. Please note, it will begin one hour earlier than the scheduled time shown on the website.

* Keynote: Prof. Qiang JI from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, NY, USA
-- Knowledge-Augmented Deep Neural Networks for Facial Action Detection

* Keynote: Dr. Xunbing Shen from Jiangxi University of Chinese Medicine
-- Micro-expression Recognition and Deception Detection

* FME'21 challenge and workshop will be held via a hybrid mode, i.e. jointly onsite in Chengdu, China and online.

* FME'21 challenge belongs to the ACM MM'21 Multimedia Grand Challenge track, and the activity will take place at October 23, 2021, 14:00-16:40 pm (UTC+8). (ACM Multimedia 2021 Program at a Glance)

* Registration website is available now. Please read carefully the instruction before registration.(https://2021.acmmm.org/registration)

* The GitHub links for the top three winning spotting methods and generation methods are available, please refer to the spotting and generation result page.
